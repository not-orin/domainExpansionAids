# Ethical Framework

[//]: # (stealing this from the system admins code of conduct)
## Personal Integrity;
- Be honest and forward about competence.
- Seek others assistance when needed.
- Strive to achieve a high-quality output to the best of your ability.

## Communication:
- Communicate with anyone on matters of mutual interest.
- Talk openly about any and all potential issues.
- Ensure that all members involved are included and understand your ideas.

## Responsibility:
- Make sure to build and maintain a safe, healthy, and productive workplace.
- Make decisions consistent with the safety, privacy, and well-being of the community and public.
- Accept honest criticism and act on user feedback.
- Credit others contributions and creative works which are used.
- Take responsibility for the work you create and the effects it has on other people and the wider world.
